App name: Cannonfire

App description: A 3d environment where the user is able to fire projectiles at objects in the environment. The user can control the velocity of the projectile and it's impact on the target.

Environment: medium/large map size. shadows, lighting, textures
Environmental physics: Collision, gravity, impact physics

Projectile: Small black sphere
Target: cubes of random mass and size
Number of targets: 9

Crossair: Small red cross fixed in the center of the users view

Input control: aim using mouse, full control of view. Fire using left mouse button
Velocity control: hold fire button to increase power and speed of projectile

Impacted target: Velocity of the projectile and mass of the target affects the impact of the targets physics
